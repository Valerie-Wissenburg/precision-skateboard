The Team

Phil Burcher

Owner and overlord of Precision Skateboards

Lee Osterholt

Lee is an elusive dude. We know his name, and that he likes cars, but after that things start to get fuzzy. Also, he is allergic to denim.

Matthew Ratliff

Lovingly referred to as 'Ratnuts' or 'Ratsy', Matthew is the Team manager, and helps keep things under control.

Drew Newlin

An Omaha native, Drew is the shop connection to fame. As part of the Mike Smith crew, and nephew to a member of 311, Drew adds cool points on and off the board.

Derek Driver

When Derek isn't busting out cool t-shirt or skateboard designs, he is busy destroying any transition he can find!

Logan Young

Logan keeps it busy- he likes to make music, skate his backyard ramp, and film and edit skate videos!

Andrew Windel

Andew is a ball of positivity and I-dont-give-a-fuck additude. Long standing shop homie, and b-team rider, Windel has moved up to the ranks of full team rider!

Steve Andel

As a skate celebrity, Steve went by the name Venom, and made it into all the magazines. Now-a-days Steve is a full time photographer. You are most likely to find him cruising around on his bike, or stalking hawks.

Jared Godfrey

As Precision's resident vegan, Jared keeps the chill factor high. As a technical wizard on the board Jared can be seen dancing on the nearest ledge when he's not busy saving lives as a nurse.

Sean Souders

Sean is the world traveler on the team. Recently returning from a stint in Europe, Sean spends most of his time in school, where he is finishing up his degree.

Jeremy Knowles

As the youngest and newest team member Jeremy is ready to hit the ground running, and the team films their first video!

David Maya

Joining the team the same time as Jeremy, David is equally ready to make things happen. Keeping the chill factor high, David can be found asking for a ride, as he doesn't drive. He just never got into it. Anyway, you'll be there soon right?
